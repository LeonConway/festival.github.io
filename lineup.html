CAFUNE

Cafune

Cafuné is an American indie pop duo formed in 2014 by singer-songwriter Sedona Schat and producer Noah Yoo. The two met at New York University. Their single "Tek It", from their debut album Running (2021), went viral, and they were subsequently signed to Elektra Records in 2022.

DV4D

dv4d

David Anthony Burke (born March 28, 2005), known professionally as D4vd,[a] is an American singer and songwriter. In 2022, he released the singles "Here with Me" and "Romantic Homicide", which gained widespread popularity on TikTok and resulted in him signing with Darkroom and Interscope Records.

MANESKIN

ManeSkin

Måneskin is an Italian rock band formed in Rome in 2016. The band is composed of lead vocalist Damiano David, bassist Victoria De Angelis, guitarist Thomas Raggi, and drummer Ethan Torchio.

CREEPY NUTS

Creepy Nuts

Creepy Nuts are a Japanese hip-hop duo consisting of DJ Matsunaga and R-Shitei, currently signed to Onenation and Sony Music Associated Records.

thxSoMch

thxSoMch

Carter De Filippis, known professionally as ThxSoMch, is a Canadian singer-songwriter . From Toronto, he started his career when he released his first single, "Runaway" in September 2022

UPLIFT SPICE

Uplift Spice

THE MUSMUS, formerly known as UPLIFT SPICE, is a 4 piece emo rock band. They formed as UPLIFT SPICE in 2005 and disbanded on September 27, 2014, but returned as THE MUSMUS in September 2015 and have been active since.
